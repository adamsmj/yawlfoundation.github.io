FoPAS 2020

Paper Submissions

The FoPAS 2020 workshop invites full papers (8-10 pages in the IEEE-CS format) describing mature research. In addition, short/position papers (4-6 pages in the IEEE-CS format) describing preliminary results and ongoing research, industry project reports and cases studies are welcome.

The paper selection will be based upon the relevance of a paper to the main and related topics, as well as upon its quality and potential to generate relevant discussion. All contributions will be peer reviewed based on the complete version, being full or short.

All submissions should be made in PDF format and comply with the IEEE Computer Society Conference Proceedings Format Guidelines. The proceedings will be published by the IEEE Computer Society Press and be made accessible through IEEE Xplore and the IEEE Computer Society Digital Library.

Please submit your paper to Easychair at: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=fopas2020

At least one author of each accepted workshop paper will have to register for the EDOC 2020 conference and attend the workshop to present the paper. If a paper is not presented in the workshop, it will be removed from the workshop proceedings published in the IEEE Xplore digital library.
